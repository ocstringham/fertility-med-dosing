This website is designed to assist fertility nurses calculate doses for common fertility medicines.
The information calculated is:
  • The date the patient finishes their medicine
  • How much more medicine you might need to order or how much is left over
The information the user provides is:
  • The current amount of medicine on hand
  • The dosage per day
