This website is designed to assist fertility nurses and patients with calculations for common fertility medicines.
The information calculated is:
  • The date the patient will finish their medicine
  • How much more medicine needs to be ordered
The information required is:
  • The current amount of medicine on hand         
  • The dosage per day
